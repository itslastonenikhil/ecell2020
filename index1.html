Hello There

We Are E-Cell, IIITG

E-CELL IIITG is a body that encourages the students of Indian Institute of Information Technology, in becoming entrepreneurs. We help the students, in making their ideas a reality. We support the students in shaping their start-up and getting them close to many entrepreneurs for making this journey of “learning to be an entrepreneur ” a successful one. The basic concept of our entrepreneurship cell is to give a basic insight into the world of entrepreneurship and provide a helping hand in any way possible to the interested.
